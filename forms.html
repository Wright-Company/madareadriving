I will obey all driving laws. I will observe and abide by posted speed limits. I will obey all rules established by my parents and will drive the car only with their permission. If I break the rules, I will forfeit the car keys with no argument or debate. Refusal to do so will result in the loss of my driving privileges.

I further agree to the following:

  • I will not drive when I am, or ride with anyone who is, tired, angry, or using any “substance”, including medications, or is in any other state that may impair driving abilities.
  • I will wear my seatbelt at all times and will not drive unless all passengers wear theirs.
  • Under no circumstances will I allow myself to become distracted, including the use of a cell phone, or any other electronic device, unless I am safely stopped off of the road.
  • I will respect all weather and road conditions, slowing down as needed for safety.
  • If I’m involved in a crash, no matter how minor, I will not leave the scene without the permission of police officers and my parents.
  • I will inform my parents about any tickets, crashes and encounters with police (including warnings.)
  • I will not have any passengers or allow anyone to drive the car without specific permission.
  • All trips will be to agreed upon locations discussed beforehand. I will not make any unscheduled stops, side trips or changes without parental contact.
